Our Services

Balcony Safety Nets

Balcony safety nets are installed to prevent accidental falls from high-rise buildings, especially protecting children and pets. They are made of durable, high-quality nylon or HDPE material that ensures strength and long-lasting support. These nets provide safety without blocking air or light, maintaining proper ventilation. They act as a protective barrier for open spaces like balconies, windows, and staircases. The nets are weather-resistant and easy to maintain, making them ideal for apartments and commercial complexes. They also help in keeping birds and dust away from your balcony

Invisible Grills

Invisible grills are a modern safety solution that offers protection without compromising the view. These grills are made from stainless steel or high-tension aluminum wires, coated with anti-rust material to resist harsh weather conditions. Mostly used for balconies and windows, they ensure safety for children and elderly people. Invisible grills add a sleek and elegant look to the home while providing strong protection against intruders or accidental falls. They are commonly preferred in apartments and high-rise buildings for aesthetic safety. With minimal maintenance and high durability, they are a perfect alternative to traditional iron grills..

Cloth Hangers

Cloth drying hangers are an essential addition to every household, especially in apartments where space is limited. These hangers are ceiling-mounted or wall-mounted with a pulley system that allows easy adjustment of height. Made of stainless steel rods or durable nylon ropes, they provide a strong and long-lasting solution for drying clothes. They help in saving space while keeping the balcony or indoor area neat and organized. These systems are user-friendly and ideal for all weather conditions. Cloth drying hangers are perfect for daily laundry needs, ensuring convenience and hygiene.

Pigeon Protection Nets

Pigeon safety nets are specially designed to prevent pigeons and other birds from entering balconies, windows, and AC duct areas. These nets protect the surroundings from bird droppings, foul smell, and possible health risks. Made from UV-resistant and weatherproof material, they offer long-lasting durability even under harsh sunlight or rain. Pigeon nets are easy to install and do not obstruct air or light flow. They help maintain cleanliness and hygiene in residential and commercial buildings. These nets also act as a physical barrier to avoid nesting in open spaces.

Anti Bird Nets

Anti-bird nets are used to block the entry of birds into specific areas such as agricultural fields, factories, warehouses, and terraces. These nets are made of tough, transparent materials that do not obstruct the view but offer strong protection. They help in preventing birds from damaging crops, food storage, and machinery. Anti-bird nets are lightweight, UV stabilized, and resistant to weather changes, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor use. They ensure a bird-free environment, maintaining cleanliness and reducing noise pollution caused by birds.

Children Safety Nets

Children safety nets are designed to safeguard kids from falling off open balconies, windows, or staircases. These nets are made of strong, high-quality materials that can withstand significant pressure and weight. They provide a secure environment for children to play freely in high-rise homes or terraces. These safety nets are transparent and blend well with the surroundings, without affecting the aesthetics of the building. Parents often prefer these nets in apartments, schools, and daycare centers. They are a must-have for childproofing your home and ensuring complete safety.

Cricket Practice Nets

Cricket practice nets are essential for players to train safely and effectively within a confined area. These nets are made of high-tensile nylon or HDPE material that can handle the impact of cricket balls. They are commonly used in schools, academies, sports grounds, and backyards for professional and amateur practice sessions. The nets help prevent the ball from going out and ensure continuous play. With flexible design and easy installation, they are suitable for both indoor and outdoor practice. Cricket nets are also used for coaching and training drills.

Duct Area Nets

Duct area safety nets are installed in open duct spaces between buildings to prevent objects, waste, or birds from falling through. These nets are mostly used in apartments where air conditioning units or wiring is placed in common duct areas. They protect the area from dust, birds, and accidental falls. The nets are made from UV-stabilized materials to withstand tough weather conditions. Duct nets are durable, lightweight, and low maintenance, making them a practical safety feature. They help in maintaining hygiene and safety in residential and commercial complexes.

Coconut safety Nets

Coconut safety nets are specially designed to prevent coconuts or dried branches from falling in areas near coconut trees. These nets are installed in residential areas, walkways, parking spaces, gardens, and public places to avoid injury or damage caused by falling coconuts. Made from high-strength, UV-resistant materials, these nets are weatherproof and can withstand the weight of heavy coconuts. They provide a protective shield while maintaining airflow and visibility. Coconut safety nets are easy to install, durable, and require low maintenance. They ensure public safety and are a smart solution for areas with tall coconut trees.
